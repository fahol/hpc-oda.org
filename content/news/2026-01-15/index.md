The HPC Operational Data Analytics community is launching the **HPC ODA TechTalk Series**, a new public presentation series featuring invited talks from practitioners and researchers across the HPC community.

The series evolves from the community's long-running internal brownbag sessions into a more open format: presentations are made publicly available, including slides and recordings, so that the broader HPC community can benefit from the knowledge shared.

Each session features a 30-minute presentation followed by 30 minutes of open discussion and Q&A. The series covers use cases, experiences, best practices, operational data engineering, AI and machine learning applications, and more.

The inaugural TechTalk will be announced shortly. If you are interested in presenting or would like to be notified of upcoming sessions, please reach out through our [contact page](/#contact).
